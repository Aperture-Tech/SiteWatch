# Privacy Policy for SiteWatch

**Effective date:** 1 July 2026

SiteWatch ("the app") is a website-monitoring app published by **ApertureTech
LLC** ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data the app handles. In short:
**the app has no backend, no account system, and collects no personal data.**

## Data the app collects

**None that is sent to us or anyone else.** SiteWatch has no servers and we
receive no data from your device.

Everything you enter or the app generates — the websites you add, their labels,
check intervals, captured page snapshots, and your notification history — is
stored **only locally on your device**, in the app's private storage. It never
leaves your device except as described under "Network access" below.

## Network access

To detect changes, the app connects **directly from your device to the websites
you choose to monitor** in order to download their content. These connections go
to those third-party websites, not to us. The app does not use
analytics, advertising, tracking, or any third-party SDKs.

The content of the sites you monitor is subject to those sites' own privacy
policies and terms.

## Permissions

- **Internet / Network state** — to fetch the pages you monitor and to run
  checks only when a connection is available.
- **Notifications** — to show local alerts on your device when a monitored site
  changes. Notifications are generated on-device; nothing is sent externally.

## Data sharing

The app does not share, sell, or transmit your data to us or any
third party.

## Data retention and deletion

Your data lives only on your device. You can delete it at any time by removing
individual sites, clearing notification history in the app, or uninstalling the
app (which removes all app data).

## Children

The app is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect any personal
information from anyone.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the same location
with a new effective date.
