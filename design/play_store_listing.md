## Full description (max 4000)

```
SiteWatch keeps an eye on the web pages you care about and tells you the moment
they change — a price drop, a new announcement, a status flip, a fresh event
listing. Set it once and let it run.

Everything happens on your device. There's no account, no cloud, and no data
leaves your phone. SiteWatch simply visits the pages you choose, checks them on
your schedule, and sends you a local notification when something is different —
and now tells you WHAT changed.

WHAT YOU CAN MONITOR
• Full page — get alerted on any change to a page's visible content.
• Specific text — know when a word or phrase appears or disappears (e.g. "In
  stock", "Registration open").
• CSS selector — watch one element, like a price or a status badge, and see the
  exact old → new value.
• Visual — detect when the top of a page simply looks different.

SEE WHAT CHANGED
Notifications don't just say "something changed" — they summarize it: the text
that was added or removed, or the value that went from one thing to another.

BUILT FOR EVERYDAY USE
• Add as many sites as you like, each with its own label.
• Choose a check interval per site.
• Pause or resume any site with one tap.
• "Check now" for an instant, on-demand check.
• A history of every change, so you never miss one.
• Clean, modern Material You design with light and dark themes.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• No sign-up, no login, no tracking, no ads.
• No analytics or third-party SDKs.
• All your sites, snapshots, and history stay in the app's private storage on
  your device. Uninstalling removes everything.

GOOD TO KNOW
• Background checks run roughly every 15 minutes at most (an Android system
  limit for battery-friendly background work); use "Check now" any time for an
  instant check.
• SiteWatch fetches the pages you add, just like a browser. Please monitor
  responsibly and respect the terms of the sites you watch.

Perfect for tracking product restocks, price changes, ticket and registration
openings, job postings, event updates, or any page that quietly changes when
you're not looking.
```
